Past Arrabiata

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 teaspoons red pepper flakes (depending on your desired level of spiciness)
  • 1 28-ounce can of crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
Instructions:
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until it is al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta cooking water before draining the pasta.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes and saut√© for 1-2 minutes, or until the garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes to the skillet, stirring to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the arrabiata sauce. Toss to combine, adding reserved pasta water as needed to thin the sauce to your desired consistency.
  5. Serve the pasta hot, topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh basil.
  6. Enjoy your spicy and flavorful Pasta Arrabiata!
